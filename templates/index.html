About us

Fitness2DA is a fitness trainer software solution that helps users perform postures correctly during their workouts by providing real-time feedback on the poses they are performing. It features a user-friendly interface that allows users of all fitness levels to track their progress. It also allows users to customize their workouts and track their progress over time.

Fitness2DA is like a personal coach that is always available to provide guidance and support. It provides personalized feedback and allows users to track their progress and make adjustments accordingly.
